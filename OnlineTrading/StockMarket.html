What is Stock Market?

A stock market is a financial marketplace where stocks, bonds, and other securities are bought and sold. It provides a way for companies to raise money by issuing stocks and for investors to earn a return on their investments. The stock market is also a barometer of the overall health of an economy, as the performance of the market is closely tied to the performance of the economy as a whole.
